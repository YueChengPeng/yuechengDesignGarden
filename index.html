Featured Projects

MOISTVALVE

A Moisture Sensory Soft Valve

Material Design | Soft Sensors | Ergonomics

Overview: From the technology perspective, I explore the possibilities of using smart responsive materials in the pneumatic context. This work presents a smart soft pneumatic valve that is water-responsive. It can realize logic control based on environmental humidity degree without the need for electricity, hard sensors or computer control.

Personal Project, Jul 2022 - Oct 2022

WHEN WIND BLOWS

A Visual Story of an Outrageous Social Event

Data Visualization | Social Issue

Overview: A data visualization of public opinions on social platforms after a video of a mother of eight being locked with a chain around her neck went viral. Through this work, I want to call for more awareness and actions towards human trafficking and many other social issues alike.

Personal Project, Feb 2022 - Jun 2022

48°C SUGAR

Material Redesign of Traditional Sugar Figure Blowing Art

Material design | Human Food Interaction | User Research & Testing

Overview: To preserve and revive the sugar figure blowing art, I want to attract GenZ by allowing them to learn about this tradition through hands-on experience. Traditionally, this art features a steep learning curve, and thus I design a new sugar material with a lower glass transition temperature that makes this art easier to learn for novices. This new material allows safe and easy diy experience and brings about many interesting interaction capacitites.

Personal Project, Aug 2022 - Oct 2022

MAGPAPER

Cheap and Reconfigurable Rapid Prototyping of Haptic Interface Based on Paper

Personal Fabrication in HCI | Material Design | Rapid Prototyping

Overview: Although haptic feeling is an important aspect of prototyping, student designers may find it difficult to incorporate haptic feedback into their demos. Based on magnetic elastomers and paper, I design an easy-to-fabricate, cheap, customizable, and reconfigurable physical interface toolkit with haptic feedback. It allows designers to easily include haptic feedback in their prototypes.

Personal Project, Nov 2022 - Dec 2022
