MOISTVALVE

MOISTURE SENSORY SOFT VALVE

Material Design | Soft Sensors | Ergonomics

Overview: From the technology perspective, I explore the possibilities of using smart responsive materials in the pneumatic context. This work presents a smart soft pneumatic valve that is water-responsive. It can realize logic control based on environmental humidity degree without the need for electricity, hard sensors or computer control.

Personal Project, Jul 2022 - Oct 2022
